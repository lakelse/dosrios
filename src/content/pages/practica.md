## What's a Practica?

A practica is an open practice session - less formal than a milonga, more structured than dancing at home. There are no tandas, no cortinas, no codes. You come to work on your dancing.

## What Happens

Show up any time during the session. Find a partner, work through something specific, take a break, try again. Experienced dancers are happy to talk through technique if you ask.

**May Special: Technique Class included**

Throughout May, a technique class runs from 8:00 to 9:00 PM as part of the practica. No registration needed - just show up.

## Who's It For

All levels. Beginners are especially welcome - the Monday practica is a good place to work on what you've learned in class, or to explore the basics with a patient partner.

## What to Bring

- Clean, smooth-soled shoes (or proper dance shoes)
- Comfortable clothes you can move in
- Water
