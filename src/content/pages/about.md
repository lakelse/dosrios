## Who We Are

Dos Ríos is a community of Argentine tango dancers based in Winnipeg, Manitoba.
We embrace both traditional and nuevo styles, and organize milongas, practicas,
and classes to bring tango to the city - welcoming dancers of all levels.

## The Styles

**Traditional** - danced to the classic orchestras of the Golden Age:
D'Arienzo, Pugliese, Di Sarli, Troilo. Close embrace, structured ronda, tandas and cortinas.

**Nuevo** - an open, exploratory approach to the dance that draws on
the same vocabulary but invites more improvisation, open embrace, and contemporary music
including Piazzolla and electronic tango.

Many of our events are mixed - check the style tag on each event listing to know what to expect.

## What We Do

**Milongas** - social tango dances. Traditional milongas follow ronda
etiquette with tandas and cortinas; nuevo milongas are more open in format.

**Practicas** - open practice sessions where you can work on technique,
ask questions, and dance informally across both styles.

**Classes** - instruction for beginners through to experienced dancers,
covering technique, musicality, and connection in both traditional and nuevo.
