Argentine tango has a way of finding people. Maybe you caught a performance, or a friend dragged you to a class, or you stumbled into a milonga by accident and stayed until the last cortina. However it happened - welcome.

Dos Ríos is a home for tango in Winnipeg. We're building a community around the dance: regular practicas, milongas, classes for all levels, and the conversations that happen in between.

## What to Expect

We're keeping things simple to start. Practicas where you can work through things without pressure. Milongas with traditional ronda etiquette - tandas, cortinas, the cabeceo. Classes that take technique and musicality seriously.

## Join the Discord

The Discord server is where the community lives between dances. Event announcements, music recommendations, questions, conversation. If you're curious about tango in Winnipeg, that's the place to start.

See you on the floor.
