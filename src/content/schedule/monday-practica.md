Open practica every Monday from 6:30 to 9:00 PM. All levels welcome.

The format is informal - work through things with a partner, ask questions, or just dance. Experienced dancers are always happy to share what they know.

**May Special: Technique Class included**
Throughout May, a technique class runs from 8:00 to 9:00 PM as part of the practica. No registration needed.

**No practica May 18** — Victoria Day holiday. The RA Steen Community Centre will be closed.
